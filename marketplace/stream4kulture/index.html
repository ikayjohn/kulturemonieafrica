Why It Matters

Streaming should not extract value from creators.

Stream4Kulture is designed to keep cultural value circulating closer to the artists, rights holders, and communities generating it, while linking audience activity to broader funding and participation flows.

Core Model

Every stream supports more than content playback.

  • Local ownership strengthens economic sovereignty around African content
  • Fair monetization improves how revenue reaches creators and rights holders
  • Creator-first distribution prioritizes sustainable earnings over platform extraction
  • Token rewards connect streaming activity to participation and funding layers
