Featured Programs

Festival

KultureRhyde Mobile Festival

Africa's largest Entertainment Citizen Carnival — activating 10 creative industries across 10 days, connecting 10 million entrepreneurs and influencers into a ₦250 billion culture capital marketplace.

Diplomacy

Kulture3rybeFace

A continental culture diplomacy movement building heritage envoys who translate creativity into sustainability, youth innovation, and economic empowerment aligned with Agenda 2063.

Technology

KultureRoodie SmartTab

Africa's biggest Tech4Kulture mobility platform — deploying culture-powered smart devices that convert data access into structured creative finance, health inclusion, and green participation.

The Culture Content Creators Carnival — mobilizing 10 million digital storytellers to transform narrative power into economic power.
